Henk Geveke is Vice-Chair of the Executive Board at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences (AUAS), where his portfolio includes digitalisation, artificial intelligence (AI), educational operations, and key enabling technologies. Previously he held senior leadership positions at the Dutch National Police, the Netherlands Organisation for Applied Scientific Research (TNO), and the Dutch Ministry of the Interior and Kingdom Relations. He also serves on the Executive Board of the Amsterdam AI Coalition.
