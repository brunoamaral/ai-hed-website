Raúl Casado Linares is a Senior Lecturer at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences, where he teaches courses in global value chains and technology. Before joining academia, he spent the majority of his professional career in the corporate sector, holding various roles as a financial and business analyst and consultant. In recent years, his work has focused on generative AI projects, particularly the application of this technology in educational contexts, including research, tutoring, and assessment.
