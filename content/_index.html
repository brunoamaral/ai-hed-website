What is the AI-HED project?

We aim to adjust teaching and learning environments in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) to the shifts caused by Artificial Intelligence.

This project focuses on enhancing skills, developing AI-integrated courses, and creating recommendations for HEIs to adopt AI responsibly and inclusively.
