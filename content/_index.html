What is Artificial Intelligence in Higher Education (AI-HED)?

AI-HED aims to adjust teaching and learning environments in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) to the shifts caused by Artificial Intelligence.

This project focuses on enhancing skills, developing AI-integrated courses, and creating recommendations for HEIs to adopt AI responsibly and inclusively.

Explore our key activities

AI-HED focuses on empowering educators and students while driving innovation in higher education.

AI Literacy Resources

Starter kits, training materials, and MOOCs to enhance AI literacy among educators.

Pilot Courses

Development and evaluation of 16 pilot courses integrating AI across various disciplines.

Outreach Activities

Engaging stakeholders to share project results and best practices.

Recommendations

Guidelines for HEIs to responsibly integrate AI in teaching and learning.
