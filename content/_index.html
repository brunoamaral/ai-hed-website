What is Artificial Intelligence in Higher Education (AI-HED)?

AI-HED aims to adjust teaching and learning environments in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) to the shifts caused by Artificial Intelligence.

This project focuses on enhancing skills, developing AI-integrated courses, and creating recommendations for HEIs to adopt AI responsibly and inclusively.
